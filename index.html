The Midnight Botanica line

The Midnight Botanica line gathers your favourite herbs into elegant prints, journal pages and recipe cards: lavender, mullein, damiana, blue lotus, chamomile, peppermint and more. Each design treats the plant as both symbol and story.

These pieces are created as décor and journaling companions, not as medical advice. Always research herbs and speak with a professional before using them in your own body rituals.

Concept card · Midnight Botanica

“Balanced” night blend

  • Lavender · for softening the edges.
  • Mullein · for clear lungs and clear intentions.
  • Damiana · for warmth, mood and sensual courage.
  • Blue lotus · for dreams and liminal thought.
  • Chamomile · for unwinding without disappearing.
  • Peppermint · for brightness and fresh focus.

In the artwork, each herb is illustrated with its correspondences and a small ritual prompt so you can build your own tea, bath or altar blend intentionally. Research all herbs and your own health needs carefully before use.

About the studio

Midnight Garden by Murphy Made

Midnight Garden by Murphy Made is built for women who bloom in the dark: exhausted, evolving, strategic and soft all at once.

Murphy Made is the parent company; Midnight Garden is the first daughter brand: a print-on-demand studio where structure meets spellwork. Every piece begins as a quiet sketch at night and ends as a made-to-order object in your hands.

  • Designed and curated in small batches under Murphy Made.
  • Printed on demand to reduce waste and keep collections rare.
  • Rooted in dark feminine aesthetics, botanical mysticism and old-money restraint.
  • Built for witches, soft villains, notaries, mothers, artists and anyone rewriting themselves at midnight.
