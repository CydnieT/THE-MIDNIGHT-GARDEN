The maker

The woman behind the Garden

I'm Cydnie, the creator of Midnight Garden — an ordained minister, Kentucky notary signing agent, and designer of dark feminine tools for intentional living. This studio was built for women like me: exhausted and evolving, soft and strategic, done with chaos but not done with themselves.

Midnight Garden is where structure meets ritual. Every planner, journal, and page is designed to hold both your humanity and your magic: the bills and appointments and to-do lists, but also the grief, the rebirth, the shadow work, and the quiet little miracles.

Visually, the Garden speaks in black velvet, antique gold, celestial geometry, roses, and thorns. It's for those of us who bloom in the dark and don't feel the need to explain that to anyone.

  • Ordained minister & ceremony writer
  • Kentucky notary & loan signing agent
  • Digital studio owner & designer
  • Builder of rituals for women who are tired but not finished
